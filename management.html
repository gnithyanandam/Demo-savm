Message from Management

Sri Annamma Devi Vidya Mandira Trust founded in 2003 started a school Sri Annamma Devi Vidya Mandira in the same year , drawing inspiration from Swami Vivekananda and Sir M Vishweshwarayya with a motto "Service to man is service to God” and with a motive of providing education to the under privileged. In the beginning there were only 21 students. Now, it has reached around 300 students with a very well equipped infrastructure. It is our unwavering commitment to the highest quality education that has speeded us on this journey. We would also like to thank the generations of parents who have entrusted us with the care and education of their children. It is their faith and belief that has helped us come this far.

Sri Annamma Devi Vidya Mandira Trust was founded with the guiding principle: "Service to Man is Service to God."

We ensure to provide a solid foundation to the kids who will be equipped both in terms of skills and values enabling them to choose their choice of higher education, benefitting to build their career. Along with the requisite academic and related skills, we try and to instill in our students empathy for those less fortunate and concern for the environment around them.

We believe education is the cornerstone of national development. At SAVM, we strive to equip students with academic excellence, strong values, empathy, and social responsibility. We sincerely thank all parents who have trusted us with their children’s future.Together, we are shaping a brighter tomorrow.
