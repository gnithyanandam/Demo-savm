  • History & Legacy

Sri Annamma Devi Vidya Mandira was established on 18th May 2003 at Park Road, Kumara Swamy Layout 2nd Stage, Bengaluru. The institution was founded under the inspiration of Sri Chandrashekariah, Founder of Vijaya High School, and through the dedicated efforts of Late Shri S. Krishnappa, Founder President.

Starting with classes from 1st to 5th standard, the school expanded gradually. In 2008–09, it was upgraded to an English Medium High School, duly recognized by the Government of Karnataka, offering education up to 10th standard.

The first SSLC batch appeared in 2011–12, and since then 14 batches have successfully passed out with 100% results, reflecting academic consistency and excellence.

Key Milestones

  • 2003: School established with classes from 1st to 5th standard.
  • 2006: New building inaugurated with four classrooms.
  • 2009: Auditorium, Library & Science Lab added.
  • 2012: Expansion into a modern two-storey campus with Smart Boards & AV facilities.
  • 2025: Launch of Redberries Nursery Wing, designed exclusively for early learners
