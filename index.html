Educational Excellence

Balanced Growth

At SAVM English Medium School, we nurture the whole child mind, body, and character through a balanced curriculum that blends strong academics, creative arts, physical development, and essential life skills.

Learn More

Core Values

  • Honesty
  • Respect
  • Responsibility
  • Kindness

Honesty

Members of the SAVM community tell the truth and act with integrity and honor. We do not mislead, cheat, steal, or lie.

Learn More

Respect

Members of the SAVM community affirm the intrinsic dignity of all people. We act with tolerance, courtesy, and thoughtful regard for all persons, for property, for the environment, and for ourselves.

Learn More

Responsibility

Members of the SAVM community are reliable and hold themselves and others accountable for their actions. We do not make excuses, blame others, or take unwarranted credit.

Learn More

Kindness

Members of the SAVM community exhibit caring and compassionate behavior in all aspects of daily life. We are not mean, and we do not harass, nor act in a cruel manner. By our positive example, we discourage unkind behavior in others.

Learn More


Mission

To establish a unique educational institution that imparts knowledge infused with patriotism, cultural awareness, and moral values, ensuring total personality development—especially for children from economically disadvantaged communities.

Vision

To build a brighter future through honest and dedicated efforts by providing affordable, value-based, and holistic education, empowering every child—particularly girls—to become confident, responsible, and compassionate citizens.
